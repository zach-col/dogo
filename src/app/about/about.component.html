Founder - Monica Geller

Monica Geller from Austin, Texas had a problem. She would always go traveling for vacation or business trips and had to leave her dog at a dog Kennel daycare but wanted more for her dog.

So she came up with Dogo a place to find other dog owners who will take care of your dog while your away and treat them just like family.
